About Us

The Game Changed, So We Changed the Game.

Our Story

Basketball has always been more than a sport — it's identity, discipline, and opportunity. But for too long, players with real talent have been overlooked. Politics, favoritism, and lack of exposure created a broken system. We've lived it. We've coached through it. We've watched countless athletes fall through the cracks because they didn't have the right platform.

That's why The 3 Ball Network was born — not in a boardroom, but on the sidelines, in the gym, and in the quiet moments when we knew something had to change.

💡 Where It Started

Founded by former NBA players, coaches on all levels, and military veterans who knows what it's like to grind without a spotlight, 3 Ball started as a simple idea: What if every player had a fair shot?

  • Not just the ones with clout.
  • Not just the ones with followers.
  • Every player. Every game. Every possession.

We started small — grading film, writing scouting reports, helping kids understand their worth both on and off the court. But as the demand grew, so did our vision.

🚀 Where We're Going

This isn't just a scouting platform — it's a movement.

We're building a full ecosystem where real coaches grade games, break down film, and deliver honest feedback to help players grow.

Our current and upcoming features include:

  • Game Grades (A–F based on performance)
  • Player Analysis
  • Scouting Reports
  • Live Game Streams & Playback (Coming Soon)
  • Education: NIL, Leadership, Etiquette, Social Media Training
  • ESPN-style Stat Overlays & Heatmaps
  • 2K-style Player Ratings
  • Weekly Player Resumes
  • Coach & Scout Dashboards
  • Fan Access to Watchlists & Favorites
