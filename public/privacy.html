Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Our Commitment

At 3 Ball Network, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our basketball community platform.

Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email address, role (player, coach, scout, fan)
  • Profile Data: Basketball statistics, school information, location
  • Game Content: Video uploads, performance data, scouting reports
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our platform and services

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide personalized basketball analytics and insights
  • Connect players, coaches, scouts, and fans
  • Generate performance reports and scouting evaluations
  • Improve our platform and develop new features
  • Communicate important updates and opportunities

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including:

  • Firebase Authentication: Secure user authentication and authorization
  • Firestore Database: Encrypted data storage with access controls
  • HTTPS Encryption: All data transmitted securely
  • Regular Security Audits: Continuous monitoring and updates

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

We take special care to protect the privacy of users under 13 years old:

  • Parental consent required for users under 13
  • Limited data collection for minors
  • No behavioral advertising to children
  • Parents can review and delete their child's information

Data Sharing

We never sell your personal information. We may share data only in these limited circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal requirements
  • To protect safety and security
  • With service providers under strict confidentiality agreements

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and review your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Control privacy settings and data sharing
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

International Users (GDPR Compliance)

For users in the European Union, we provide additional protections under GDPR, including:

  • Clear consent mechanisms
  • Data portability rights
  • Right to be forgotten
  • Data processing transparency

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We'll notify you of significant changes via email or through our platform. Your continued use of 3 Ball Network after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
